 🚦 Outage Priorities & Required Actions:

 🔹 Low Priority

* Outage Size: Less than 250 kW
* Example: Combiner box outage
* Report By: Within 24 hours
* Ticket Updates: Once per week

---

 🔸 Medium Priority

* Outage Size: 250–3,150 kW
* Example: Partial inverter outage
* Report By: Within 12 hours
* Ticket Updates: Every 48 hours

---

 🔺 High Priority

* Outage Size: 3,150 kW
* Example: Full inverter outage
* Report By: Within 6 hours
* Ticket Updates: Every 24 hours

---

 🔴 Critical Priority

* Outage Size: Over 3,150 kW
* Example: Multiple inverters or total site outage
* Report: Immediately – direct notification required
* Ticket Updates: Every 6 hours

⚠️ Action:
If no work order or notification is found in Lens, send a Lens "Notify Outage via Email" for Critical Priority outages.
