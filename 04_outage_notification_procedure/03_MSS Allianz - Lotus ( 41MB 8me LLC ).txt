🚦 Outage Priority Levels & What They Mean:

* Affected Equipment: Inverter Submodules

 🔺 High Priority

* Outage Size: 1,000 to 15,000 kW
* Affected Equipment: Full Inverter or AC Feeder

 🔴 Critical Priority

* Outage Size: More than 15,000 kW
